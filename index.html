VPN Drop-Box Gateway (RPI 4)

Inspired by Mr. Robot

The Concept: Inspired by the classic tactic used by Elliot Alderson in the series Mr. Robot. The goal was to create a discreet physical device (Drop Box) that could be left connected at a target facility (building, office, etc.) to establish persistence.

This device allows the operator to leave the site and remotely connect to the target's internal network securely and comfortably, simulating a physical presence on-site.

How it works:

  • Hardware: Raspberry Pi 4 (8GB RAM) acting as a Gateway.
  • C2 Connection: Uses a reverse WireGuard tunnel pointing to an external VPS (cloud).
  • Remote Access: Enables internal audits and vulnerability scans through the VPN tunnel without exposing local ports.
Stacks: [ Raspberry Pi OS, WireGuard, VPS, Kali Linux Tools, Networking ]
